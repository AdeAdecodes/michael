About Me

Every leader has blind spot (s). Vulnerability and honesty are the grace to win inspite of them.

Michael Oguche

Michael Oguche is a Certified Performance Coach, Management Trainer, Certified Human Resource Management Professional (CHRMP), Senior Human Resource Professional International (SPHRi), Speaker, Preacher and Author.

Michael uses his expertise to help individuals and organizations to advance human capital development and performance. He has inspired a lot of professionals and others in areas of personal leadership and in the pursuit of their life purpose through training, coaching, counseling, speaking and through his books.

Michael has consulted for reputable organizations in areas of Learning and Development, Organization Restructuring, Talent Management, Recruitment, Staff Performance Optimization, Performance Coaching and Organization Culture Alignment and Transformation.

He has also designed and facilitated training programs for organizations within and outside Nigeria, with regular demand for courses like: Fortified Leadership™, Exceptional Managers™, Leading with Emotional Intelligence, Customer Centric Service, Emotional Intelligence, Public Speaking and Presentation Skills, Supervisory Skills, Innovative Talent management, Gender in Leadership, Change Management, Professional Sales and Marketing, Organization Culture, Leading Women, Problem Solving and Decision Making, Team Effectiveness, Professionalism and Productivity, Effective Communication, Building High Performance Team, Time Management and Personal Productivity and other strategic business improvement programs.

Michael is well experienced in deploying critical training assessments such as "DISC Behavioural Analysis", "SPEAR Test", "Wheel of Life Assessment" "Team Effectiveness Assessment" etc.

He is also in high demand for organizations in facilitating their Quarterly Business Review (QBR)Retreats, Business Restructuring, Design and Implement Balanced Scorecard.

He is the host of the weekly broadcast tagged, "Business of Life" where inspires a lot of people through his faith based, inspiring and mind shifting messages.
